# Welcome to the BC + AI Ecosystem 🌲🤖

**This is BC + AI: part solarpunk R&D lab, part community potluck, all heart, no gatekeepers.**

We are British Columbia's grassroots AI collective. We believe in DIY over gatekeepers, community before clout, and building a future where artificial intelligence serves culture, creativity, and the planet. From hackathons in Vancouver to art-tech jams in the Fraser Valley, we are a mycelial network of technologists, artists, researchers, and storytellers building the future together.

Our GitHub is where our community-driven tools, open-source R&D, and hackathon data live. We emphasize sharing knowledge, code, and learnings rather than siloing them off.

## 🔗 The Hub: Where We Connect

## 🛠️ What We're Building

Our repositories reflect our radically local, ethically bold approach to AI. Here is what you'll find:

- **Hackathon Archives & Templates:** We host multi-round, cross-disciplinary events like the Data Storytelling Hackathons. Check out our public datasets and projects ([`vanai-hackathon-004`](https://github.com/bc-ai-ecosystem/vanai-hackathon-004)) to see how we tackle real-world questions with AI.
- **Community Data & Transcripts:** Open-source archives of our monthly Vancouver AI meetups ([`vancouver-ai`](https://github.com/bc-ai-ecosystem/vancouver-ai)), ensuring our conversations and learnings are accessible to everyone.
- **Ecosystem Tools:** Custom bots, WhatsApp filters, and community management scripts built by our members to keep our network thriving.

## 🌱 Getting Involved

We are open to co-creating. If you want to contribute to our open-source projects or spin up something new:
